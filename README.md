# Sales Performance Analysis Looker Studio

## Project Overview
This project develops a centralized sales dashboard for Maru Footwear Sales, a footwear retailer operating across multiple e-commerce platforms including Shopee, Tokopedia, Lazada, and Blibli.

## Objective 
- Analyze sales performance across multiple marketplaces on each platform.
- Identify top-performing products and categories based on key metrics. 
- Support data-driven decision making by providing interactive insights that help optimize sales strategy, pricing, and marketplace focus.

## ToolS
Google Looker Studio

## Dataset 
Maru Footwear Sales

## Analysis Approach
- Analyzing shoe sales data from various e-commerce platforms such as Shopee, Tokopedia, Lazada, and Blibli.
- Visualizing sales performance on e-commerce platforms in an interactive Looker Studio dashboard to support data-driven decision making.

## Conclusions
- Shopee is the main revenue contributor, accounting for the largest share of total orders and showing a consistently higher revenue trend compared to other marketplaces.
- Formal category dominates total quantity sold, indicating strong demand for formal footwear across all marketplaces.
- Refund impact is relatively low, with a refund rate of 1.3%, although the total refund value still needs monitoring due to its steady increase.
- Sales performance varies significantly across platforms, showing that each marketplace has different strengths in terms of volume, revenue, and product performance.
  
## Suggestions
- Prioritize Shopee as the primary sales channel by allocating more promotions and inventory to maximize its strong revenue contribution.
- Strengthen the Formal product category through targeted campaigns and bundle offers, as it consistently generates the highest sales volume.
- Monitor and reduce refund value by analyzing refund reasons and improving product descriptions, sizing accuracy, and quality control.
